Private Markets · Liquidity Modeling / Built for the XIG Platform Management problem set

Bringing private markets to everyone created a liquidity problem nobody had to solve before.

Evergreen and semi-liquid funds promise individual investors something private assets were never built to give: the ability to leave on a quarterly basis. Hold too much cash to honor that promise and you bleed returns to drag. Hold too little and you gate redemptions in a stress quarter. This tool models the trade-off the platform team lives in — and finds the band that keeps both in check.
